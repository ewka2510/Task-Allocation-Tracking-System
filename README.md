# Task Allocation Tracking System (Weekly Shift-Based Analytics)

Designed and developed an operational analytics solution for tracking task allocation among associates based on weekly shift plans.

The project provides visibility into workload distribution, task ownership, and rotation balance across operational teams.

---

## Business Problem

Operational teams lacked clear visibility into how tasks were distributed across associates during weekly shifts.  

This made it difficult to:
- ensure balanced workload distribution
- monitor task ownership consistency
- maintain cross-training across multiple responsibilities
- reduce dependency on specific individuals for critical tasks

---

## Solution

An end-to-end reporting and analytics solution was developed to track and visualize task allocation across associates.

The workflow includes:

- processing weekly shift plan data
- mapping associates to assigned tasks
- aggregating workload distribution per agent
- building percentage-based allocation metrics
- visualizing operational balance in Power BI dashboards

---

## Overview

The overview shows:
- the percentage distribution of different task assignments for each individual agent throughout the week  
- the contribution of assigned agents to specific tasks such as Chinese Lobby, Carrier Holds Lobby, WIMS, and Lobby Sweeper  

This provides better visibility into how many people are currently relied upon for each task area and may help support more balanced task rotation.

The idea behind it is also to ensure that a wider group of agents remains actively involved in different responsibilities on a regular basis. This helps maintain familiarity and confidence across multiple tasks, increases operational flexibility, and reduces dependency on a limited number of people when others are on holiday, off sick, or otherwise unavailable.
